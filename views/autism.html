Asperger / Autism

Autism is a developmental condition that affects how a person communicates, interacts, and experiences the world around them. It is often referred to as Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) because symptoms and their severity can vary widely among individuals. Common features include challenges with social interaction, differences in communication, and repetitive behaviors or interests. Autism is typically present from early childhood and is influenced by a combination of genetic and environmental factors. While it is lifelong, many people with autism develop strategies and skills that help them thrive.
