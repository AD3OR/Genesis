Brachydactyly

Brachydactyly is a hereditary condition characterized by unusually short fingers and toes due to shortened bones. It may occur as an isolated trait or as part of certain syndromes. Types vary depending on which fingers or bones are affected. Most cases are harmless, but some may be associated with other medical conditions.

Common Symptoms

Unusually short fingers

Shortened toes or foot bones

Abnormal bone development
