Barber Syndrome

Barber Syndrome is a rare genetic condition characterized by developmental anomalies and specific physical features. It affects growth, cognitive development, and sometimes cardiac or skeletal systems. Research is ongoing to understand its genetic underpinnings and long-term impact.

Common Symptoms

Distinct craniofacial features

Delayed speech and motor skills

Cardiac irregularities
